Happy's Masters Pool

Augusta National Golf Club — April 9-12

How To Play

Pick one golfer from each of the 6 tiers below.

Your score = combined strokes of your best 4 golfers. If you don't have 4 that make the cut, you score an 80 per round for missed rounds.

Tiebreaker: Scorecard playoff starting Hole 1, final round.

Text your 6 picks to Happy!
