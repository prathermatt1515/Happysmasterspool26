Happy’s Masters Pool

Augusta National Golf Club — April 9–12

How It Works

Pick one golfer from each of the 6 tiers.

  • Rounds 1 & 2: All 6 of your golfers count toward your team total.
  • Rounds 3 & 4: Your best 4 golfers (by R1+R2 combined) advance. Bottom 2 are dropped.
  • Missed Cut: Any advancing golfer who misses the cut scores 80 per round for R3 & R4.

Lowest total score wins. Tiebreaker: Scorecard playoff starting Hole 1, final round.
